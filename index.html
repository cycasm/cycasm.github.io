Overview

The Oreiro's Garden and Landscaping Services App is an app created for our Capstone Project at STI College Rosario, Cavite. It features an ordering system for garden plants and items, project management system for landscaping projects, 3D garden designer, and augmented reality for previewing gardens, all in one Android app. It is specifically tailored for our client - Oreiro's Garden and Landscaping Services located at Tanza, Cavite.

Role

Lead Mobile Developer — responsible for UI design, Android and Unity module development, and Firebase integration.

Overview

Mobile Palengke is an application developed as part of our Technopreneurship subject. Although the project remains incomplete, particularly in terms of payment processing, administrative features, and other functionalities, its main goal was to create an ordering platform specifically designed for fresh produce sold in Philippine public markets, commonly known as "palengke". While the project have been discontinued due to challenges in fully conceptualizing and developing the idea, it remains significant to me as it was my first working Android application I built from scratch.

Role

Lead Mobile Developer - responsible for UI design, Android development, and Firebase integration.
